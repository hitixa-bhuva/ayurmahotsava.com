Call for Abstracts – Ayur Mahotsava 2025

Ayur Mahotsava 2025 invites scholars, researchers, academicians, and practitioners from across the globe to present their groundbreaking work in Ayurveda and allied sciences. This is an opportunity to share innovative ideas, research findings, and clinical advancements with a distinguished audience at one of the most prestigious platforms in Ayurveda.

Guidelines for Submission

  • The abstract should be original and unpublished.
  • Word limit: Maximum 300 words (excluding title and author details).
  • The abstract should include: Title, Objectives, Methodology, Results, and Conclusion.
  • Submissions should be in English.
  • The presenting author must register for this national ayurveda festival.
